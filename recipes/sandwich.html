Sandwich

DESCRIPTION

Making a Sandwich is quite fast and it is perfect for a quick snack

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • 1 Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup/Mustard/Mayo (optional)

STEPS

  1. Take two slices of bread from the package
  2. Spread your favorite condiment onto the bread
  3. Cut the tomato into slices
  4. Wash up the lettuce
  5. Put how much lettuce and tomato you want on the slices of bread
  6. Put the two slices of bread together and enjoy!
