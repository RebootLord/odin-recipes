PopCorn

DESCRIPTION

Making a PopCorn is quite easy and it is perfect for a quick snack

INGREDIENTS

  • A big ass pan
  • Cooking oil
  • A sack of raw popcorn
  • Salt

STEPS

  1. Put the pan on a stove and turn the fire on
  2. Then, put some quantity of cooking oil
  3. Put how much raw popcorn you want. Close the pan.
  4. Past some time, open up your pan and put your popcorn on a bucket
  5. Sprinkle a bit of salt if you want and enjoy!
