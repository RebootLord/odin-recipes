HOTDOG

DESCRIPTION

Making a HotDog is quite simple and it is perfect for a quick snack

INGREDIENTS

  • Hot water
  • Sausages
  • Bread
  • Ketchup/Mustard (optional)

STEPS

  1. Boil the water and put the sausages in the water
  2. Open the bread and put the boiled sausage inside the bread
  3. Put some condiments according to your taste, like mustard or ketchup
